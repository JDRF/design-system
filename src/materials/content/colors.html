The reverse of these colors (white text on a darker color) would be as follows:

  • Primary at 100% white: #ffffff/$text-reverse-primary
  • Secondary at 76% white: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.76)/$text-reverse-secondary
  • Disabled at 50% white: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.5)/$text-reverse-disabled
